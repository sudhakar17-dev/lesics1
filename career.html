Videographer Cum Video Editor

  • Any graduation
  • October 10, 2023

Job description :

We are seeking a talented and creative Videographer Cum Video Editor to join our team. The ideal candidate should have strong proficiency in both videography and video editing. This role will be responsible for compelling visual content and transforming it into engaging, high-quality videos that align with our company's goals and brand identity. The engineering animation team at Lesics Engineers Pvt Ltd produces videos for our YouTube channel 'LESICS'. The videos are educational in nature, so the multimedia expert should be able to create visual effects which are of engineering interest.

Responsibilities :

  • Exciting video shooting for Youtube video production.
  • Plan and execute video shoots, including selecting appropriate equipment, setting up lighting, and capturing high-quality footage.
  • Film a variety of content, including promotional videos, interviews, product demonstrations, and event coverage.
  • Ensure proper framing, composition, and lighting of shots to create visually appealing content.
  • Maintain and organise video equipment, ensuring it is in good working condition.
  • Edit and assemble raw footage into polished videos that tell a compelling story.
  • Add music, sound effects, graphics, and visual effects to enhance the quality of videos.
  • Colour correct and adjust audio levels to ensure the highest video and audio quality.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and editing software/tools to continuously improve editing skills.
  • Manage and organise video files, ensuring they are easily accessible for future use.
  • Maintain an efficient workflow for video production both pre and post production processes.

Requirements :

  • Proven experience as a videographer and video editor, with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.
  • Good skill in Panasonic Lumix GH5 Camera.
  • Proficiency in video recording equipment, lighting, and sound equipment.
  • Proficiency in video editing software.
  • Strong storytelling and creative skills, with an eye for detail.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to work and adapt to changing project requirements.
  • Knowledge of current trends in videography and video editing.
  • Basic knowledge of motion graphics.
