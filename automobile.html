How does counter steering work?

  • Sabin Mathew
  • May 16, 2022
  • 5 Comments

Before understanding the concept of counter steering, let’s first understand the concept of normal motorcycle steering, which basically leads to no-slip wheel condition. The wheels always try to attain a non-slip condition, which means that the wheels should have no relative motion with the road. But, since the road is at rest, the wheel contact point should have zero velocity. How is zero velocity possible for a moving wheel? It is possible because the wheel has two kinds of motion, a translational motion along with the vehicle, and a rotational motion on its own axis.
